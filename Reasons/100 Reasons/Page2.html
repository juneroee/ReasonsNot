100 Reasons not to commit suicide.

21.
Your scars can tell a story.

Our scars are a testimony that no matter how big the Giant was before us, the God inside us was stronger.

22.
Beaches.

There is a feeling of satisfaction every time you walk on wet sand, feel the ocean breeze on your face, and smell the saltiness of the sea. The beach always gives you the feeling of being relaxed and satisfied.

23.
Road trips.

There’s this thing about road trips that throws our sense of time out of the window.

24.
There are creatures depending on you.

What will happen to the creatures that depends on you if you're already gone?

25.
Apple’s next iPhone.

Life isn’t perfect but your Phone can be. The phone is just not the technology it’s an emotion.

26.
Look in the mirror.

If you’re searching for that one person that will change your life. take a look in the mirror.

27.
Depression is treatable.

Depression is a manageable and treatable illness. Help is right around the corner. All you need to do is take the first step.

28.
Flowers.

A flower cannot blossom without sunshine, and a man cannot live without love.

29.
You make other people happy.

Life laughs at you when you are unhappy; Life smiles at you when you are happy; But life salutes you when you make other happy.

30.
The world wouldn’t be the same.

People need you in their life when you’re not around. they feeling like a piece of them is missing.

31.
At least a small part of you wants to live.

We all know that small part of you wants to live that's why you're here looking for a reason to live.

32.
You would miss out on the better part of life.

Better days are comming but if you end your life right now you will not experience those days.

33.
The sun.

Hope is like the sun, which, as we journey toward it, casts the shadow of our burden behind us.

34.
Photographs.

If a photographer cares about the people before the lens and is compassionate, much is given. It is the photographer, not the camera, that is the instrument.

35.
New books.

Books are the quietest and most constant of friends; they are the most accessible and wisest of counselors, and the most patient of teachers.

36.
Someone is praying to meet a person like you.

When someone prays for you without you knowing. I don't think there's any form of purer love.

37.
Your future kids won’t ever make it here.

If you end your life right now, you also end the life of your future children.

38.
Cuddling with your favorite person or pet.

There is nothing more beautiful than holding your loved ones, and cuddling them in your arms.

39.
Someone in a support group could learn from your story.

Your struggle can also help someone.

40.
The 7 Great Wonders of the World.

The world's seven wonders stand the test of time: Truth, love, joy, faith, peace, virtue, and wisdom.
