100 Reasons not to commit suicide.

1.
You are important.

YOU are important! You have a unique purpose, that only you can fulfill. This post serves a gentle reminder that YOU matter! Don’t ever question that one tiny fact, YOU are very important!

2.
You are not worthless.

Why do you feel the need to believe your own lies? You need to believe that you are worth it and you need to believe that you will get through this.

3.
You matter.

You matter because you are the only person who can be you. This planet has over 7 billion people and yet you are the only person who is exactly like you. Take for instance, your fingerprints; you are the only one with them.

4.
You are NEVER alone.

You are never alone because there are people who think and feel the same way as you do.

5.
Nobody else can be you.

To be yourself in a world that is constantly trying to make you something else is the greatest accomplishment

6.
You have potential.

There is no man living who isn’t capable of doing more than he thinks he can do.

7.
Better days are coming.

It's important to be able to distinguish between what happens to us and who we are, and look forward with hope for new and better days ahead!

8.
Your loved ones will never get over it.

They will learn to live with it. they will heal and they will rebuild theirself around the loss they have suffered. They will be whole again but they will never be the same.

9.
It will leave unanswered questions.

Suicide does that. It brings sorrow and raises questions that linger. It begs for answers that rarely come.

10.
You Have a Destiny That Only You Can Fulfill.

Your dreams are your destiny and can only be fulfilled by you.

11.
YOU Are Meant to Be Great.

You are the type to continue to find ways of building yourself into your greatest version imaginable.

12.
YOU Mean the World to Someone.

Gentle delicacies like being loved by another, should never be underestimated. You have a reason to be proud.

13.
YOU are a V.I.P.

live your life like one! Hold your chin up high, puff your chest out and strive to live life at your best!

14.
Situations can change.

Maybe you messed up or made the wrong choice. But continuing your life gives you the power to take back control over the circumstances and improve them.

15.
Emotions aren’t permanent.

No matter how isolated, hopeless, angry, or lost you feel right now, you won’t always feel that way. Emotions come and go, and you can learn how to better manage them.

16.
Your pets will be lost without you.

Just remember that when you're gone your pets don't know why you never come back.

17.
Surviving is success.

Survival can be summed up in three words, NEVER GIVE UP!

18.
You can meet new people.

Nothing more exciting than meeting new people, hearing their stories, and being inspired.

19.
Someone wants to hug you.

A hug delights, warms, and charms. That must be why God gave us arms.

20.
You are talented.

Remember that you are talented and your talent has value.
