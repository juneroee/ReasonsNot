100 Reasons not to commit suicide.

61.
Your next birthday.

A Birthday is a new beginning - A birthday is the first day of another lovely year, A year of new tomorrows that is shining, A year of possibilities as endless as the sky, and a chance to meet the challenges in everything you try.

62.
Coffee.

It doesn’t matter where you’re from—or how you feel…there’s always peace in a strong cup of coffee.

63.
Cats.

Always remember your cat because time spent with cats is never wasted.

64.
You are smart.

The sexiest thing in the entire world is being really smart. And being thoughtful and being generous.

65.
You have battles to win.

The battles that count aren't the ones for gold medals. The struggles within yourself - the invisible, inevitable battles inside all of us - that's where it's at.

66.
There is someone waiting for you.

Waiting for someone is the hardest thing so appreciate that someone is waiting for you.

67.
It will cause pain, not end it.

You will just pass your pain to your loved ones not and end it.

68.
Stars.

Not just beautiful, though – the stars are like the trees in the forest, alive and breathing. And they’re watching you.

69.
New discoveries.

Many thing are not yet discover, so keep yourself alive so when new discoveries arrive you will hear, touch, or see them.

70.
You will be succesful.

Success is no accident. It is hard work, perseverance, learning, studying, sacrifice and most of all, love of what you are doing or learning to do.

71.
Suicide is not glamorous..

Suicide is not a natural response to adversity. Negative events in one’s life do not typically end with a response of suicide.

72.
Not much in life is worth dying for.

Don’t give any issue which is minor, but may feel like it’s major at the time, the credit. Your life is much too valuable.

73.
Struggles can provide meaning.

Going through the struggles of life builds character and resilience. Some of the toughest people on the planet have lived the most difficult lives.

74.
There is help available.

Individuals in helping professions spend time and money on school in order to be trained to help others.

75.
Thoughts are inaccurate.

It is impossible to know if one is a burden unless they ask someone. Much of these feelings are misconceptions.

76.
Bad thoughts and feelings pass.

The way one feels right now may be much different than they will feel tomorrow or in the near future.

77.
Concerts.

Concerts are amazing the lights, the sound, some crazy people you may found.

78.
Someone wants to kiss you.

A kiss is a lovely trick designed by nature to stop speech when words become superfluous.

79.
Chocolates

Reminiscent of childhood memories, luxury, sweetness and sensuality, chocolate is more than just a food – it is therapy.

80.
New York City.

New York is really the place to be; to go to New York, you’re going to the centre of the world, the lion’s den.
