100 Reasons not to commit suicide.

41.
Someone wants to listen to you.

Sometimes all you need is someone who is willing to listen to you.

42.
You can never watch your favorite movie again.

Imagine your favorite movie decided to have a sequel but you're not here anymore to watch it.

43.
You can learn a new skill.

The only skill that will be important in the 21st century is the skill of learning new skills.

44.
The Museum.

A visit to a museum is a search for beauty, truth, and meaning in our lives. Go to museums as often as you can.

45.
You can end toxic relationships.

A bad relationship is like standing on broken glass, if you stay you will keep hurting. If you walk away, you will hurt but eventually, you will heal.

46.
The negative thoughts are lies.

It is not always possible to do away with negative thinking, but with persistence and practice, one can gain mastery over them so that they do not take the upper hand.

47.
There is new food to try.

It’s not true that money can’t buy happiness. I mean, have you tried buying ice cream? It’s the same thing!

48.
Snowflakes.

Snowflakes are one of nature‘s most fragile things, but just look what they can do when they stick together.

49.
Dogs.

Once you have had a wonderful dog, a life without one, is a life diminished.

50.
You can learn to love again.

Never think that what you have to offer is insignificant. There will always be someone out there who needs what you have to give.

51.
Roller coasters.

The ones who don’t fear anything can enjoy the roller coaster ride to the fullest.

52.
Your next paycheck.

You work hard for your job to receive a paycheck so you need to get it.

53.
Laughing.

There is a thin line that separates laughter and pain, comedy and tragedy, humor and hurt.

54.
You can find a place where you belong.

Once you find out where you belong, you will be happy there. If you just go with the flow, you always end up exactly where you belong.

55.
You have a reason.

A reason that you're still alive is because you have your own reason and that is the most important reason to live.

56.
You can get better friends.

Each friend represents a world in us, a world possibly not born until they arrive, and it is only by this meeting that a new world is born.

57.
Shame is not fatal.

It is not easy to overcome the shame, but once you build up the confidence to not let shame hold you back you'll acheive much more.

58.
You are beautiful.

How dare the world define beauty. God defined beauty when he made you! You are beautiful, you are a true beauty just the way you are!

59.
Long drive.

Long drive, late night full volume music that give the best feel, no idea about destination, but its one of the magical moment.

60.
You can change your thought processes.

The first step in changing a thought pattern you’ve had your whole life is simply to start recognizing it.
